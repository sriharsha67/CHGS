Certified Base SAS Expert

Course Objective:SAS (Statistical Analysis System) is one of the most popular softwares used in the world of analytics & big data. SAS helps in data management, data cleaning and statistically analysing data. SAS certifications offered by the SAS Institute are highly sought after and globally recognized. Through this online training course you will learn the complete basics of the SAS software and language and learn how to manage and manipulate data. You will also get trained for the SAS Certified Base Programmer for SAS 9 certification exam conducted by SAS Institute. If you are a beginner in SAS and keen to get the Base SAS certification then this SAS certification training course is what you need to take. Scroll down for more details.

SAS Software & Base SAS Certification Exam details

  • Download the free SAS University Edition software from here to practice: http://www.sas.com/en_us/software/university-edition.html. You will also need VMWare Player or Oracle Virtual Box to be installed first on your system depending on the SAS University Edition version you download.
  • The certification exam will be held at a nearby to you Pearson VUE testing center
  • The certification exam will consist of 60-65 multiple choice and short-answer questions and you will have 110 minutes to complete it. A minimum score of 70% will be required to pass the exam
  • For more details on the exam and to register for it go to http://support.sas.com/certify/creds/bp.html#s1=3 to register for the Base SAS exam. Pay the exam fee (~US$180) and schedule the exam date as per your own convenience post the course
