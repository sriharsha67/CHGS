Certified R Programmer

Course Objective: R is growing into one of the most popular and in-demand analytics tool given its open-source & free nature, advanced analysis and visualization abilities. This R tutorial has been designed to make you proficient in implementing analytics studies in R. This is a 15 hours video based, completely online R tutorial where you can view the videos at your own pace. These videos will take you through the fundamentals of R programming for analytics in a structured manner. A problem solving based approach has been used through the R tutorial video content to provide holistic, practical and hands-on learning. Check out the course preview video on the left.

By the end of this R tutorial you shall be able to demonstrate your R skills to employers.

  • Course Curriculum
