Welcome to CHS

Career Heights Global Service Pvt Limited has evolved from its roots as the HR Function Management Company in the year 2013, with a smear vision to showcase the SMEs in an ushering mode with a whole new revolution in the Human Resource department functions in an organization and also position India as the new mecca for SMEs, building on the global trend of moving out of a silicon valley. Since its inception, CHS with its domain expertise, focused insight, and innovation has gained a reputation of being a dedicated and premium Human Resource Consulting Firm, enabling us to attain a status of being one of the listed and known HR company on the platform.
